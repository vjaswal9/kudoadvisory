About Kudo Advisory

Kudo Advisory works with organisations that are unsure where to start with AI or struggling to extract real value from it. We provide end-to-end AI advisory, covering strategy, policy, value identification, and solution delivery. Our focus is pragmatic: identifying where AI can genuinely improve performance, defining the right governance and guardrails, and delivering solutions that solve real business problems. Kudo is a Japanese word (駆動), and it represents the driving force that turns intent into forward motion. We exist to make AI move.
